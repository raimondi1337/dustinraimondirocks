About Bomb Chomper

Due to a recent Chrome update it takes several minutes to load. Works as intended in Firefox still.

Bomb Chomper is a simple arcade game made in HTML 5 Canvas made by Rob Prorok and myself. Use the arrow keys to direct your ship and prevent the bombs from hitting Earth. You lose when the Earth has take too much damage.

Technologies or Techniques Used

  • HTML 5 Canvas
  • Javascript
  • Time Based Animation
  • Basic Collision Detection
